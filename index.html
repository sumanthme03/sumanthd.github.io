Welcome

Hi! and welcome to my website. My name is Sumanth Reddy Dadam. I am a Senior Research Engineer in Ford Motor Company. Previously, I was a Research and Development Engineer at the Bosch Technical center, India. I obtained my Masters from the Department of Mechanical Engineering, where I was advised by Prof. Jerry Ku [Thesis].

Before joining WSU, I spent 5 beautiful years at Bosch and Caterpillar UK

I am broadly interested in the fields of Optimization, Distributed Inference and Statistical Machine Learning. I am interested in applying techniques related to optimization towards Robust Deep Learning and Learning/Optimizing on Edge Devices, i.e., Federated Learning.
